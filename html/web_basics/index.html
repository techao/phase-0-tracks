What are dogs?

They are little (or big) creatures that I consider to be much cuter than cats

There are countless types of dogs and personalities they may have depending on what suits you as a petowner. Here are just a small list of the type of dogs.

  • Great Dane
  • Maltese
  • Golden Retriever
  • German Shepard

And there are also reasons that I choose to have a dog rather than a cat

  1. I am allergic to both but my dog is hypoallergenic
  2. Dogs are extremely loyal
  3. Dogs are also very playful
  4. Dogs just want to love and be loved
